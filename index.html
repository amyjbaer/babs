What is B.A.B.S.?

B.A.B.S. is a smart dice roller that lets you control how often sevens appear in your rolls. Perfect for board games where you want to reduce the frequency of certain outcomes!

How It Works

Normally, rolling two dice gives you a 16.67% chance of getting a seven. With B.A.B.S., you can:

  • Limit sevens in a rolling window - e.g., max 2 sevens per 3 rolls
  • Control overall percentage - keep sevens at or below your target
  • Track detailed statistics - see how your rolls compare to natural probability
